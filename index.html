❓ Frequently asked questions
How does PickMyAI work?
PickMyAI asks you 5 quick questions about your goal, budget, skill level, industry, and workflow preferences. It then uses Google Gemini AI to match you with the 3 tools best suited to your exact situation — not generic top-10 lists.
Is PickMyAI free?
Yes, completely free. No account required, no credit card, no limits. PickMyAI is free forever.
How many AI tools are in the database?
PickMyAI has analysed over 500 AI tools across writing, coding, automation, marketing, research, video, customer support, data analysis, productivity, HR, and finance categories.
Are the recommendations unbiased?
Yes. Recommendations are generated by AI based purely on your answers. We do not accept payment for placement in results. Our only goal is to find you the right tool.
Can I share my results with someone?
Yes! When you get results, just copy your browser URL or click "Share results". Your answers are encoded in the URL so anyone who opens it sees the same recommendations.
What if none of the recommendations fit?
Click "Start over" and try different answers. You can run the matcher as many times as you like — maybe try a different goal or skill level to see alternative recommendations.
