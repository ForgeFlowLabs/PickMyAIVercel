Our story

We built PickMyAI because we were tired of the noise.

Every week, dozens of new AI tools launch. Every week, the same listicles appear — "Top 50 AI tools you need right now." Every week, people waste money on subscriptions that don't fit their actual workflow.

PickMyAI started with a simple idea: what if the recommendation was personalised to you, not to everyone? Your goal, your budget, your skill level, your industry. Five questions. Three tools that genuinely fit.

"The businesses and people who get the most value from AI aren't the ones who adopted the most tools — they're the ones who adopted the right ones."

Our principles

🎯
Precision over volume

Three perfect tools beat a list of fifty mediocre ones. We'd rather give you less and have it be exactly right.

🤝
No paid placements

We don't accept payment to appear in results. Recommendations are based purely on fit — not who has the biggest affiliate programme.

🌍
Accessible to everyone

AI tools should be findable by anyone — not just people who already know what they're looking for. PickMyAI is free, forever.

🔍
Honest about limitations

AI recommendations aren't perfect. When a tool has real trade-offs, we say so. You deserve the full picture.
