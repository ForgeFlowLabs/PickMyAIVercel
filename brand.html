Brand Guidelines

The PickMyAI
Brand Guide

Everything you need to represent PickMyAI consistently — colours, typography, voice, and logo usage.

01 — Mission

Who we are

The foundation everything else is built on.

Mission statement

To cut through AI overwhelm and help every person find the right tool for their exact situation — instantly.

Vision

A world where anyone can harness AI — regardless of technical skill, budget, or industry.

Values

Clarity over noise. We strip away hype and give honest, specific recommendations.

Precision over volume. Three perfect tools beat a list of fifty mediocre ones.

Accessibility over gatekeeping. AI tools should be findable by everyone, not just experts.

Trust over traffic. We'd rather give an honest recommendation than a sponsored one.

02 — Logo

The mark

The logo is a gem-like icon paired with the Cormorant Garamond wordmark. Always use them together. Never stretch, recolour, or separate them.

Logo rules

Minimum size: Never display the logo smaller than 24px tall.

Clear space: Always maintain at least the height of the gem icon as clear space on all sides.

Don'ts: Don't rotate, skew, apply drop shadows, change the font, or place on busy backgrounds without a container.

06 — Voice & Tone

How we speak

PickMyAI sounds like a knowledgeable friend — honest, calm, and precise. Never hyped, never condescending, never vague.

We do this

Write short, confident sentences

Give specific, honest reasons for each recommendation

Acknowledge trade-offs (e.g. "more powerful but pricier")

Use plain language anyone can understand

Speak directly to the user ("you", "your")

Lead with the most important information

We don't do this

Use AI buzzwords like "cutting-edge", "revolutionary", "game-changer"

Over-promise ("the best tool ever made")

Add unnecessary exclamation marks

Use passive voice or corporate-speak

Recommend sponsored tools without disclosure

Be vague ("great for many use cases")

Tone examples

❌ Don't say: "Discover our revolutionary AI-powered matching engine that will transform how you interact with cutting-edge technology!"

✅ Do say: "Answer five questions. Get three tools matched to your exact situation."

❌ Don't say: "This amazing tool has tons of features that many users find super helpful!"

✅ Do say: "Excels at long-form content — ideal if you're writing blogs or email sequences regularly."

Border radius: Always 100px (fully rounded) for buttons and pills. 16–24px for cards.

Padding: 13–15px vertical, 24–34px horizontal for full buttons. 6–8px vertical, 14–20px horizontal for small pills.

Hover state: translateY(-1px to -2px) with increased box-shadow. Never change background on gradient buttons — use opacity instead.

Spacing system

Section padding: 96px vertical on desktop, 64px on mobile.

Card padding: 32–44px on desktop, 22–28px on mobile.

Gap between elements: 8px (tight), 16px (standard), 24px (relaxed), 40px (section breathing room).

Max content width: 940px for sections, 600px for the matcher card, 480px for email/centred content.

08 — Imagery

Visual style

PickMyAI is a digital-first product. We rely on colour, typography, and UI to communicate rather than stock photography. When imagery is needed, follow these principles.

If using photos

People: Real, candid, diverse. Never staged stock smiles. Show people actually working — at desks, on laptops, in creative spaces.

Colour treatment: Apply a subtle lavender or violet colour grade to keep images on-brand. Avoid warm-heavy or high-saturation edits.

Mood: Calm, focused, premium. Think editorial magazine, not startup cheerleader.

Avoid: Generic "AI brain" graphics, neon robot imagery, cliché "holographic hands touching screens" stock photos.

Iconography

Style: Use emoji for option icons inside the product (approachable, universal). Use simple SVG line icons for UI controls and navigation.

Size: 18–22px for inline emoji in options. 14–16px for SVG UI icons.

Colour: SVG icons inherit text colour. Never use multi-colour custom icons — keep them monochrome.

09 — Brand Don'ts

What to avoid

Protecting the brand is as important as building it.

Don't use the word "revolutionary" or "disruptive" — PickMyAI solves a real problem quietly and confidently.

Don't use competitor names negatively — We don't need to put others down. We let results speak.

Don't use dark patterns — No fake urgency, no misleading pricing, no hidden affiliate bias in recommendations.

Don't let the gradient appear on text longer than 4 words — Gradient text is a premium accent, not a default text treatment.

Don't place the logo on gradients or busy backgrounds without a white or dark solid container behind it.

Don't mix fonts — Only Cormorant Garamond and DM Sans. No exceptions.
